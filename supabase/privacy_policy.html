Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Threefold ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by Outpace Strategy Group. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Threefold mobile application.

Information We Collect

When you use Threefold, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your email address and encrypted password when you create an account.
  • Profile Information: Your name, identity type, identity statement, focus areas, and coaching style preferences provided during onboarding.
  • Goal Data: Daily goals, completion statuses, reflections, notes, and sleep ratings you enter in the app.
  • Chat Messages: Conversations with the AI coach feature.
  • Push Notification Tokens: Device tokens used to send you reminders.
  • Contact Information: When you use the "Phone a Friend" feature, we access contacts you explicitly select. We do not store your full contact list.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Threefold app experience, including goal tracking, streaks, and insights.
  • To deliver AI-powered coaching and personalized recommendations based on your profile and goal history.
  • To send push notifications for daily reminders (morning and evening check-ins).
  • To sync your data across devices via cloud storage.
  • To facilitate the "Phone a Friend" feature, allowing friends to suggest goals via a web link.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For authentication, database storage, and serverless functions. Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: Your AI coach messages are processed by OpenAI's API (GPT-4o-mini) to generate coaching responses. Messages are sent to OpenAI's servers for processing. OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Expo: For push notifications delivery. Device push tokens are processed through Expo's notification service. Expo Privacy Policy

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage for offline access and synced to Supabase cloud servers for backup and cross-device access. Authentication sessions are stored securely using your device's secure storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android).

We implement row-level security policies to ensure you can only access your own data. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us or using the "Delete Account" option in the app.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Children's Privacy

Threefold is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
